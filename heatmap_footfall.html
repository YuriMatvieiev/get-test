How to use Footfall Heat Map

Step 1

Begin by selecting an area of interest, such as a city, neighborhood, or specific street

Step 2

Analyze the color-coded map, which visually represents footfall activity, to identify areas with a significant volume of pedestrian traffic.

Step 3

Make informed decisions for your business or marketing strategies by leveraging the insights gained from the footfall heat map, such as selecting high-traffic locations for store openings or planning targeted advertising campaigns.

Mapping UK Footfall Patterns

The Footfall - Pedestrian Traffic - Heat Map layer provides valuable insights into the movement and flow of people within the cities of the UK.

By selecting this data layer, you can access visual representations of footfall patterns, allowing you to identify areas with high pedestrian activity.

Whether you're a retailer looking for prime locations, a marketing professional seeking to target specific audiences, or an urban planner interested in optimizing infrastructure, our Footfall Heat Map offers essential information to make informed decisions.
