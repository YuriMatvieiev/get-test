Discover Location Insights

Footfall & Vehicle Traffic

Number of people walking and driving by the location

  • How many people walk by the potential location?
  • How many cars drive by it?
  • What is the distribution of traffic throughout the workday? Weekend? Week? Month?

    • Knowing the footfall and vehicle traffic is the key to estimating the number of people who could potentially be exposed to the brand and making revenue predictions.

Demographics

Age, gender, relationship status, household income, and more

Take a look
  • Who are the people living and working around that location?
  • Do they fit my target audience?
  • How many people are there within a 100 yards radius? 500? 1000?

Demographic data will help you make sure that there is a sufficient pool of potential customers around the target location. We bridge multiple sources of data and collate it all in one place.

Amenities

Accessibility of the location and points of interest

  • How accessible is the location?
  • What kind of supermarkets and groceries stores are nearby?
  • Are there any educational facilities nearby, such as nurseries, schools, or universities?

    • Knowing amenities around your location will help understand which kinds of people are going to be visiting these places and how best to position your establishment to be visible to them.

Competitors

Competitors, their type, distance from location, rating, number of reviews, and more

Take a look
  • What competition will my business have to face?
  • What do their customers think about them?
  • Are the big brands there?
  • Are there enough competitors around to drive people to that location?

    • Competitive analysis will help to paint a clear picture of the surroundings, understand what makes this location tick and identify hidden opportunities.
